Consider Making Honorarium & Memorial Donations to Camelid Research

Individuals who make extraordinary contributions to the camelid community can be honored by making a tax deductible contribution in their name. Send a donation and a brief description of why you want to acknowledge them. Your message and name will be listed on the Honorarium page.

Loved ones and friends we lose can be remembered by making a tax deductible donation for research in their name. Send a contribution and brief biography of your friend or family member. The bio and your name will be listed on the Memorial page.
