Events

Connect, Learn, and Support the Herd

NWCF hosts annual events and educational programs that bring camelid owners, breeders, and veterinarians together across the Pacific Northwest. The research we fund leads to better health outcomes for llamas and alpacas throughout the region.

Our events are the foundation's primary source of funding. Every dollar raised goes directly to camelid veterinary research and scholarships, with no overhead. When you attend, you are investing in the health and well-being of every camelid in the Pacific Northwest.

Annual Events

Annual Fundraiser Events

Each year, NWCF hosts fundraiser events that are the primary source of funding for our research grants and scholarships. These events are open to all camelid owners, breeders, veterinarians, and anyone who cares about the health and well-being of llamas and alpacas.

Our fundraisers have included silent auctions featuring fiber arts, camelid equipment, veterinary products, and experiences donated by our generous community of supporters. All proceeds go directly to camelid veterinary research at OSU and WSU and to the maintenance of the OSU research herd.

What to Expect

  • Silent auction with donated items from breeders, vendors, and supporters
  • Camelid demonstrations and showcase animals
  • Research updates from OSU and WSU scientists
  • Networking with veterinarians and owners from across the region
  • Q&A with the Research Committee
Education & Community

Meet & Greet Events

Our Meet & Greet events are at the heart of what NWCF does. They bring together llama and alpaca owners, veterinarians, and researchers for an afternoon of real conversation, shared knowledge, and genuine community.

These gatherings are informal by design. Whether you are a longtime owner with specific health questions or someone new to camelids who wants to learn more, you will find experienced vets and researchers ready to talk. It is the kind of access that is hard to find anywhere else.

Beyond the education, these events are a chance to meet people who share your passion. Friendships form, contacts are exchanged, and the network of people committed to camelid health grows a little stronger every time we gather. Proceeds from each event go directly toward funding the veterinary research at OSU and WSU that benefits the whole camelid community.

Vet Q&A Sessions

Get your camelid health questions answered by experienced veterinarians from OSU and WSU in a relaxed, accessible format.

Research Updates

Hear directly from the researchers your donations support. Learn what is being studied, what has been discovered, and what is next.

Community Connection

Meet other llama and alpaca owners from across the Pacific Northwest. Share experiences, exchange contacts, and build lasting relationships.

Outreach

Industry Conferences & Shows

NWCF members and board representatives regularly attend regional and national camelid shows and conferences, including events hosted by the Alpaca Owners Association, the Llama Association of North America, and state-level organizations across the Pacific Northwest.

These events are an opportunity to distribute educational materials, share information about our research funding, and connect with supporters who share our commitment to camelid health.

If you are involved with a regional camelid organization and would like to partner with NWCF on an educational event or program, we would love to hear from you.
