Honorarium & Memorial Donations

Individuals who make extraordinary contributions to the camelid community can be honored with a tax-deductible donation in their name. Send a donation and a brief description of why you want to acknowledge them.

Loved ones and friends we lose can be remembered by making a tax-deductible donation for research in their name. Send a contribution and a brief biography — both will be listed on the Honorarium & Memorials page.
