What We Do
Three Ways We Advance Camelid Health

Fund Research

We award grants to qualified veterinary researchers at OSU and WSU, supporting studies in infectious disease, immunology, reproduction, and nutrition. Results are published and shared with owners and veterinarians everywhere.

$820,000+ invested since 1987

Award Scholarships

Each year we award scholarships to veterinary students at OSU and WSU who are passionate about camelid medicine. Scholarship recipients go on to practice and publish, multiplying the impact of every gift.

$36,000+ awarded to date

Educate Owners

Through Meet & Greet events and the annual conference at OSU's Carlson College of Veterinary Medicine, we connect llama and alpaca owners with leading camelid veterinarians across the Pacific Northwest.

Events open to all owners

"Regardless of the size of their herd or the location of their barn, every camelid and every owner benefits from the research funded by the NWCF."

— North West Camelid Foundation

Why Your Support Matters

Every donation goes directly to veterinary research and scholarships. No overhead. No administrative fees. Pure impact.

  • 100% of fundraiser proceeds fund research Every dollar raised at NWCF events goes directly to science — no administrative overhead taken out.
  • Research partnerships at OSU and WSU Your gift sustains NWCF's work with two of the nation's leading camelid veterinary programs, including the OSU research herd.
  • Scholarships train tomorrow's specialists Scholarship recipients go on to practice camelid medicine and publish research — compounding your investment for years.
  • Research benefits every herd, everywhere Findings are published and shared with camelid owners and veterinarians across North America — not just in the Pacific Northwest.

How to Get Involved

There are several ways to support the NWCF's mission — from making a gift to attending an event near you.

  • Make a Donation One-time or recurring gifts fund research and scholarships year-round. All donations are fully tax deductible.
  • Attend an Event Join our annual fundraiser, Meet & Greet gatherings around the Pacific Northwest, and the biennial OSU Camelid Conference. Events are open to all owners.
  • Spread the Word Share our mission with fellow camelid owners, breeders, and veterinarians. Awareness is its own form of support.
  • Leave a Legacy Consider a planned gift or endowment to sustain camelid veterinary research in perpetuity. Contact us to learn more.
