What We Do

Fund Research

We award grants to OSU and WSU researchers studying infectious disease, reproduction, and nutrition in camelids. All findings are published and shared freely with owners and veterinarians.

$820,000+ invested since 1987

Award Scholarships

Annual scholarships support veterinary students at OSU and WSU who specialize in camelid medicine. Recipients go on to practice and publish — multiplying the impact of every gift.

$36,000+ awarded to date

Educate Owners

Meet & Greet events and the biennial OSU Camelid Conference connect llama and alpaca owners with leading camelid veterinarians across the Pacific Northwest.

Events open to all owners

"Regardless of the size of their herd or the location of their barn, every camelid and every owner benefits from the research funded by the NWCF."

Why Your Support Matters

  • 100% of fundraiser proceeds fund research Every dollar raised at NWCF events goes directly to science — no overhead deducted.
  • Partnerships with two leading vet schools Your gift sustains research at OSU and WSU, including OSU's dedicated camelid research herd.
  • Results shared across North America Published findings benefit camelid owners and veterinarians everywhere, not just the Pacific Northwest.

How to Get Involved

  • Make a Donation One-time or recurring gifts fund research and scholarships year-round. All donations are fully tax deductible.
  • Attend an Event Join our annual fundraiser, regional Meet & Greet gatherings, or the biennial OSU Camelid Conference.
  • Spread the Word Share our mission with fellow owners, breeders, and veterinarians.
